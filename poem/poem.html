Exercise Science, Movement, and Motivation

I am an exercise science major who is passionate about health, movement, and physical activity. Studying exercise science has helped me understand how the body responds to exercise and why staying active is so important for both physical and mental well-being. One of my favorite ways to stay active is running, which helps me stay focused, energized, and consistent.

My Interests and Motivation

Music plays a big role in my workouts because it keeps me motivated and makes exercise more enjoyable.
I also love spending time at the beach, where being active feels relaxing and refreshing. My favorite color is purple, which I associate with creativity and calm energy  especially during recovery days.

Why I Chose Exercise Science

  • It focuses on improving overall health and wellness
  • It explains how the body adapts to physical activity
  • It supports an active and healthy lifestyle

My Ideal Fitness Routine

  1. Start with a dynamic warm-up
  2. Go for a long run while listening to music
  3. Cool down and stretch near the beach

A reliable resource I use to learn more about fitness and exercise is the ACE Fitness website , which provides science-based information about health and training.
